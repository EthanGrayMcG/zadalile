About Me

Who am i?

I'm Zada Lile, an Actress

Zada is a 15 year old ginger with a big imagination! When not involved in performances including singing, dancing and acting, her favorite hobby is cosplay. She currently has almost 23k followers on her TikTok channel. Zada is a member of the Professional Theatre Co, Four Corners Musical Theatre, and DHS Troupe 1096. She just completed a run of Matilda the Musical as Matilda and Annie the Musical as Annie. Zada plans to pursue a BFA in Musical Theatre in college and continue in a professional acting career.
